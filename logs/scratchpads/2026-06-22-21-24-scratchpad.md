3. **Rila Monastery dropped** — parked for a future central-Bulgaria trip (with Plovdiv/Koprivshtitsa).
4. **No chairlift at Seven Rila Lakes** — hike up/down (~1.5–2h, ~550m), share-taxi/hitch the access road. Cuts the lakes day from ~€60 to ~€25 (chairlift ~€16 + private taxi ~€30 were the budget-killers). Day governed by transport curfew: last chairlift ~16:30, last cheap Dupnitsa→Sofia bus ~17:50 (arrive Sofia ~19:30). Operator OK up to ~22:00 (taxi fallback if later).
5. **Thursday departure to Bansko** (was Wednesday). Adds a 3rd Sofia night → Wed Jun 24 = full Sofia work day (fixes the tight 25h Mon–Thu work week). Net: a night moves Bansko→Sofia, cost-neutral (total fixed at 7 nights by the Mon 17:00 flight).
6. **Pirin hike = Banderishki Lakes (moderate)** over the strenuous Sinanitsa/Muratovo (~14–18km/700–900m, long+rocky). Banderishki ~8–12km/~400m — right load after a travel day and before Sunday's Seven Lakes. Sinanitsa kept as an "if feeling strong" upgrade.
7. **Both lake hikes kept** — Banderishki (Fri) + Seven Rila Lakes (Sun), split by Sat travel. Profile note: Pirin is the better crowd-fit (quiet); Seven Lakes is the signature but Sunday-crowded.
