Getting between stops

All legs are overland buses except the booked flights out. Times and prices are approximate — buy the longer tickets a day ahead, and confirm the final Ohrid → Sofia haul before the day.

Furgon-style minibuses (like the Prizren → Prevallë mountain run) leave when full, not on a timetable — so allow flex on those.
