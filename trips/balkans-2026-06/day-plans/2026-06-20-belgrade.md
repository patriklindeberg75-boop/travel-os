# Belgrade — Saturday, 20 Jun 2026 — full day

**Morning**

- [Gardoš / Millennium Tower — Zemun, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Gardo%C5%A1%20Millennium%20Tower%20Zemun%20Belgrade)
  Old tower for the Zemun-side Danube view, then the swan promenade below · ~45 min · **optional**
  → 5 min walk

- [Grill 'Loki' — Zemun, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Grill%20Loki%20Zemun%20Belgrade)
  Legendary street window — big pljeskavica with kajmak and chopped onions · quick · **must-do**
  Tip: eat it standing. Ask for kajmak on the side for extra. Šopska salata (white sirene cheese + tomato + cucumber) at any sit-down kafana nearby.
  → ~30–35 min (bus 84 or CarGo/Yandex taxi) southwest to Ada Ciganlija

**Midday — swim & shade**

- [Ada Međica — Sava island (Čukarica), Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Ada%20Me%C4%91ica%20Belgrade)
  Small boat to a car-free forested river island — wild swimming, shade, away from the crowds · half-day · **must-do**
  Tip: boat crossing from Ada Ciganlija's western shore; bring small change in dinars. No facilities — bring water and snacks.

- [Ada Ciganlija (Makiš side) — Ada Ciganlija (Čukarica), Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Ada%20Ciganlija%20Maki%C5%A1%20Belgrade)
  The quieter locals' swim stretch of the lake, not the crowded strip · backup / combined destination · **must-do**

← Rest pocket: stay as long as you want. Unplanned afternoon.

**Afternoon (when you head back)**

- [Gavez Club — Savamala, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Gavez%20Club%20Savamala%20Belgrade)
  Bohemian tavern in urban greenery — shade, dirt-cheap drinks · **optional**
  → ~20–25 min transit back toward Savamala

**Evening**

- [Splavs on Savski kej — Savski kej (Novi Beograd), Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Splavs%20Savski%20kej%20Novi%20Beograd%20Belgrade)
  Ultra-budget sunset beer on a local river barge, no tourists · **optional**
  → short walk across the bridge

- [Belgrade Waterfront (Beograd na vodi) — Savamala, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Beograd%20na%20vodi%20Savamala%20Belgrade)
  Flat evening stroll — polished but pleasant at dusk · ~30 min · **optional**

- [Zaokret — Savamala, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Zaokret%20Savamala%20Belgrade)
  The meet-people spot — 25–35 backpacker and local crowd, non-touristy, cheap drinks · **optional**

**Night (if energy allows)**

- [20/44 — Sava riverbank (Brankov most), Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=20%2F44%20Sava%20riverbank%20Belgrade)
  Cult alternative floating club — late-night, non-tourist, the real Belgrade river party · **optional**

## Optional add-ons

- [Nikola Tesla Museum — Vračar, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Nikola%20Tesla%20Museum%20Vra%C4%8Dar%20Belgrade) — Tesla's ashes + live demos, AC, indoor. English tours usually 10:00 / 12:00 / 14:00 / 16:00. Better as a Sunday morning standalone — Vračar is off today's route.

- **Dedinje / embassy area** `[extra — not from your list]` — The "fancy embassy neighborhood" is Dedinje: leafy hilltop ambassador villas, quiet streets, Museum of Yugoslavia (Tito's mausoleum) in the park. Broadly on the route toward Ada — the taxi can go via Topčider.

- **Aviation Museum** → save for Monday: right by the airport (Surčin). Perfect last-morning stop before the 14:00 departure — Cold-War jets in a dome hangar.

- [Silosi — Dorćol (old port / docks), Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Silosi%20Dor%C4%87ol%20Belgrade) — repurposed Danube grain silos with open-air art · works if you end up back in Dorćol at sunset.

**Lunch →** Grill Loki (already the must). Šopska salata at any kafana next door. Kajmak comes on the meat; ask for extra on the side.
**Afternoon →** Bring snacks to Ada — nothing on the island.
**Dinner — pick one:**
- [Čedić — central Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=%C4%8Cedi%C4%87%20central%20Belgrade) — massive cheap Serbian portions. Recommended if you're hungry after swimming.
- [Splav Koliba — Sava riverbank, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Splav%20Koliba%20Sava%20Belgrade) — traditional food on a food barge. Best if you stay riverside all evening.
- [Radecki — central Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Radecki%20central%20Belgrade) — fishermen's club, cheap river fish. Best if you want a break from grilled meat.
