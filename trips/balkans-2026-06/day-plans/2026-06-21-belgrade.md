**Morning — market + breakfast (cool hours, before the heat)**

- [Bajloni Pijaca — Dorćol, Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bajloni%20Pijaca%20Dor%C4%87ol%20Belgrade)
  Local green market — ultra-cheap white cheese, cured meats, fruit · ~45 min · **must-do** (your white-cheese craving)
  Tip: ask a dairy stall for **beli sir** (white cheese), add a scoop of **kajmak** + fresh bread — that's the classic Serbian breakfast plate. Sunday markets wind down by early afternoon, so come before ~11:00 for the full spread.
  → 2 min walk for a hot pastry next door

- [Pekara Petrović — central Belgrade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pekara%20Petrovi%C4%87%20Belgrade)

  The locals' cleaner, quieter swim stretch of Sava Lake · **must-do**
  Water is ~22–24°C this week against ~31°C air — the proper cooldown.
  Tip: **aim to hit the water after ~14:00** — that's the dossier's sweet spot (14:00–18:00) and keeps you out of the peak-midday UV window; stretch the Košutnjak shade through late morning rather than rushing straight down to the lake. The Makiš/far side is calmer and less crowded than the main beach strip; carry a few dinars for a kiosk lunch or a rented lounger, and shade up between swims — your UV sensitivity, not the heat, is the limiter.

← **Rest pocket** — this *is* your midday rest. Swim, lie in the shade, long lakeside lunch. Unplanned; stay as long as it's good.
