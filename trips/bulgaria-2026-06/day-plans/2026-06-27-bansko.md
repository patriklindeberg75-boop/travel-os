# Bansko — Saturday, 27 Jun 2026 — hike day (~3–4h hike + recovery)

> Bansko Bus Station → Vihren Hut: **08:30 · 13:20 · 17:00.**
> Vihren Hut → Bansko: **09:30 · 14:20 · 18:00.** ~10 leva (~€5) one-way.
> **Your plan: take the 08:30 up, come back on the 14:20** (back in Bansko ~15:10).

> it strands you at altitude into peak afternoon-storm hours. **Catching the 14:20
> back is the real deadline.** Source caveat: the full timetable came from a 2022
> seasonal listing, but a 2026 source gives identical up-times — still worth a
> 10-second confirm, since a missed afternoon bus is a long problem.

> 1. **Trail food + 2L+ water** — no reliable water source until the lakes; chalet

---

**Morning**

- **In town first (before the shuttle):** confirm shuttle times, buy trail food +
  water, and pre-order kapama at Zehtindjieva Kashta if you want it tonight.
- [Bansko Bus Station — Bansko](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bansko%20Bus%20Station%20Bansko)
  Catch the **08:30 shuttle** up to Vihren Chalet (~€5, arrives ~09:20). This is the
  one to make — it sets up a return on the 14:20.
  → ~50 min by shuttle up the Bunderitsa road (16 km)
- [Baikushev's Pine — Pirin NP, Bansko](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Baikushev%27s%20Pine%20Pirin%20Bansko)
  1,300-year-old Bosnian pine — Bulgaria's oldest tree. Free 5-min roadside stop on
  the way up · **optional**
  → on the shuttle route; ask the driver to pause
  Tip: it's right on the road to the chalet — no detour, just ask.
- [Vihren Chalet (Bunderitsa staging base) — Pirin NP, Bansko](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Vihren%20Chalet%20Bunderitsa%20Bansko)
  Your altitude start point — check trail conditions, fill water, grab a soup or tea
  (~€2–4) · quick · **must-do (logistics hub)**
  → trail to Banderishki Lakes starts at the chalet
  Tip: check conditions here before committing to the route above.

---

**Midday**

- [Banderishki Lakes — Pirin NP, Bansko](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Banderishki%20Lakes%20Pirin%20Bansko)
  Lower Pirin lakes circuit — quiet, teal alpine water, no chairlift crowd. Route it
  as an **out-and-back to the lower lakes and turn around to keep it ~3–4h** · **must-do**
  → Hike: ~8–12 km full circuit (your 3–4h version is shorter) · ~400 m gain ·
  water on route · **no mobile signal** · last shuttle back = verify
  Tip: download Mapy.cz **offline** before you leave Bansko — dead signal up there.
  Tip: turn back at the lower lakes once you hit your time budget — you don't have to
  close the full loop to get the payoff.
- **Off the ridges before ~14:00** — afternoon thunderstorms are the Pirin pattern.
  Start your descent in good time even if the weather looks fine.

---

**Afternoon**

- Shuttle back down to Bansko — **target the 14:20 from Vihren Hut** (back ~15:10). The 18:00 is fallback only.
- **Rest pocket** — shower, recover, cool down. A cold **tarator** (cucumber-yogurt
  soup) is the classic post-hike reset; **Garash cake** at an old-town bakery if you
  want something sweet.
- **Open pocket** — leave this unplanned. Old-town wander, sit in a café, or nap.
  Don't fill it.

---

**Evening**

- [Zehtindjieva Kashta — Novia Grad, Bansko](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Zehtindjieva%20Kashta%20Novia%20Grad%20Bansko)
  The primary Bansko mehana, off the gondola tourist strip — kapama + clay-pot
  dishes, live folk music some evenings, social. ~€7.50–10 · **must-do (if you
  pre-ordered)**
  → ~10–15 min walk from the old town into the Novia Grad side streets
  Tip: if you didn't pre-order kapama, you can still go for the clay-pot dishes —
  just ask what's ready.
- **Eat-this-here tonight:** Kapama (the Bansko speciality), Tarator (cooling),
  Garash cake.

### Optional add-ons (only if energy permits)

- [Velyan's House (Velyanova Kushta) — Old Town, Bansko](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Velyanova%20Kushta%20Old%20Town%20Bansko)
  Finest National Revival house in Bansko — frescoes, carved ceilings. ~€2–3 · quick.
  Best at 09:00 to beat tour groups, but it's a short late-afternoon stop too.
- [Dedo Tase — Old Town, Bansko](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Dedo%20Tase%20Pirin%20Street%20Bansko)
  Lower-key dinner alternative if you'd rather skip the mehana scene — village
  eatery, vegetable sach. ~€5–7.50.

### Skip for another day

- **Sinanitsa / Muratovo Lakes** — the flagship full-day circuit (~14–18 km). Parked
  for Sunday — too long for today's 3–4h target.
- **Vihren Peak** — serious 2,914 m summit, full-day, exposed ridge. Wrong load for
  today.
- **Demyanitsa Valley** — flat valley walk; your rain/rest-day backup, not needed on
  a hike day.
- **Baba Vuna / Banski Han** — daytime canteen + station mehana; better on a non-hike
  day when you're in town at lunch.
